The Scanbot SDK provides ability to scan and extract content from barcodes and QR codes.

The following barcode formats are currently supported:

#### 1D barcodes
- EAN_13
- EAN_8
- UPC_A
- UPC_E
- CODE_39
- CODE_93
- CODE_128
- ITF (Interleaved 2 of 5)
- CODABAR

#### 2D barcodes
- QR_CODE
- DATA_MATRIX
- AZTEC
- PDF_417
